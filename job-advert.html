We’re Hiring: Postgraduate Researchers (MSc / PhD) — Project IMBAS

Location: TOBB Economics and Technology University, Ankara, Türkiye

Enhancing speech recognition in challenging acoustic conditions is crucial for effective communication, especially for individuals with hearing impairments.

Project IMBAS is a large-scale research project investigating how different spoken languages are affected by acoustic maskers. The goal is to improve multilingual speech intelligibility by creating a training dataset for Machine Learning (ML) and Deep Learning (DL) algorithms.

The project is funded by TÜBİTAK 1001 – The Scientific and Technological Research Projects Funding Program.

Your role:

  • Conduct and analyze experiments on speech intelligibility under masking
  • Collect and preprocess acoustic and speech data
  • Contribute to publications and scientific reports
  • PhD track: Lead independent research and mentor MSc researchers

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in architecture, interior architecture, linguistics, acoustics, speech & hearing sciences, or related fields.
  • PhD track: Master’s degree and currently enrolled in a PhD program
  • Solid knowledge of research methods
  • Excellent English (written & spoken)
  • Experience with data analysis (Python, R, MATLAB, etc.) is a plus
