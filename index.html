Kivanc Kitapci Acoustics Research Lab

Who are we?

Kivanc Kitapci Acoustics Lab at the TOBB Economy and Technology University is a group of acoustics researchers based in the Faulty of Architecture, but we have members across the University. Our research interests revolve around speech acoustics and speech signal processing. Clich here for detailed information on the people involved.
